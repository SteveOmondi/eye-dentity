# Marketing Plan for AI Website Builder Platform

## Overview
This plan outlines a go-to-market strategy for launching and scaling the AI-powered website builder platform targeting professionals and small businesses.

---

## 1. Target Audience

- Individual professionals:
  - Lawyers
  - Doctors
  - Interior designers
  - Software developers
- Small businesses across industries
- Freelancers and consultants
- Entrepreneurs launching new ventures

---

## 2. Marketing Channels

### Organic & Paid Channels
- **Instagram**: Visual templates, testimonials, reels
- **Facebook**: Community building, ads, retargeting
- **LinkedIn**: Professional outreach, B2B targeting
- **Google Ads**: Search and display campaigns
- **YouTube Shorts**: Quick demos and success stories

---

## 3. AI Automation for Posting

- Claude agent generates:
  - Weekly content calendar
  - Profession-specific posts
  - Visuals using templates
  - Hashtags and captions
- Posts scheduled via:
  - Meta Business Suite
  - LinkedIn API
  - Google Ads API

---

## 4. Weekly Budget Enforcement

- Budget set per channel (e.g., $50/week/channel)
- Claude agent monitors:
  - Spend vs budget
  - ROI per campaign
  - Adjusts bids and frequency

---

## 5. Reporting Dashboard

- Metrics tracked:
  - Impressions
  - Click-through rate (CTR)
  - Conversion rate
  - Cost per acquisition (CPA)
  - Sales vs marketing spend
- Reports sent:
  - Weekly summary via email
  - Daily alerts via Telegram

---

## 6. Conversion Tracking

- Pixel integration:
  - Facebook Pixel
  - Google Analytics
  - LinkedIn Insight Tag
- Funnel stages:
  - Visit → Signup → Payment → Deployment

---

## 7. Cross-Sell Opportunities

- Resume download upsell
- Company profile export
- Email marketing add-on
- Advanced SEO (location-based)
- Premium templates
- AI blog generator

---

## 8. Value Additions

- Free trial with limited features
- Referral program with credits
- AI SEO included in all plans
- Profession-specific onboarding
- Custom domain branding
