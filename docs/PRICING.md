## ğŸ¤” FAQ

### Why are there payment processing fees?

Payment processing fees cover the cost of secure payment handling through Paystack. These fees are industry-standard and much lower than traditional payment gateways.

### Can I avoid the fees?

No, but you can minimize them by using M-Pesa or Kenyan cards (1.5% + KES 25) instead of international cards (3.9% + KES 100).

### Are there any hidden fees?

No! The price you see at checkout is the final price. All fees are clearly displayed before payment.

### Can I get a discount?

We offer:
- **Annual payment discount:** Pay for 12 months, get 2 months free
- **Referral bonus:** Refer a friend, get 1 month free
- **Startup discount:** 50% off first 3 months for registered startups

### What happens if I cancel?

- No cancellation fees
- Service continues until end of billing period
- Prorated refunds for annual payments (minus processing fees)
