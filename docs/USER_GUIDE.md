## Creating Your Profile

You have two ways to create your professional profile:

### Option 1: Form Mode (Traditional)

Fill out a structured form with your information:

1. **Personal Information**
   - Full name
   - Profession (select from dropdown)
   - Email address
   - Phone number (optional)

2. **Bio & Services**
   - Write a brief bio about yourself
   - Add your services (click "+ Add Service")
   - List your specializations

3. **Branding**
   - Upload your logo (recommended: PNG, 500x500px)
   - Upload a professional photo (optional)
   - Choose your brand colors

### Option 2: Chat Mode (AI-Powered)

Have a conversation with our AI assistant:

1. Click "Switch to Chat Mode"
2. Answer questions naturally in the chat
3. The AI will extract your information automatically
4. Review and confirm the collected data

**Tips for Chat Mode:**
- Be conversational and natural
- Provide specific details about your services
- Mention your location if relevant
- You can provide your own AI API key for privacy

1. Go to "My Account" → "Subscription"
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm upgrade
5. Pay prorated difference

1. Go to "My Account" → "Subscription"

## Tips for Success

### Profile Creation

- **Be specific**: Detailed information creates better content
- **Professional photo**: Use a high-quality headshot
- **Clear services**: List specific services, not generic categories
- **Compelling bio**: Tell your story authentically

### Domain Selection

- **Brand consistency**: Match your business name
- **Easy to spell**: Avoid complex words
- **Short and memorable**: Aim for under 15 characters

### Template Selection

- **Match your industry**: Choose templates designed for your profession
- **Consider your audience**: Professional vs. creative
- **Color psychology**: Colors convey different emotions

### After Launch

- **Share your website**: Add to email signatures, business cards
- **Update regularly**: Keep your profile and services current
- **Monitor performance**: Check analytics (Premium plan)
- **Collect feedback**: Ask clients for testimonials
