About Mario Rossi

My name is Mario Rossi, and for two decades, I’ve been immersed in the world of plumbing. What sets Mario Rossi Plumbing apart? It’s a combination of unwavering reliability, transparent communication, and a genuine commitment to doing the job right the first time. When you choose me, you're not just hiring a plumber; you're gaining a trusted partner dedicated to the long-term health of your plumbing system, backed by two decades of proven expertise.

Professional Leak Detection

Hidden water leaks can lead to significant property damage, mold growth, and skyrocketing water bills. Our advanced leak detection services utilize cutting-edge technology to accurately locate even the most elusive leaks, minimizing disruption and preventing further harm to your home's structure and foundation.

  • Prevent costly structural damage and mold growth.
  • Significantly reduce your monthly water bills.
  • Accurate, non-invasive detection saves time and money.
  • Protect your home's foundation and overall integrity.

Expert Pipe Repair & Replacement

From minor drips to burst pipes, issues with your home's piping can cause immediate headaches and long-term problems. Mario Rossi Plumbing offers expert pipe repair and replacement services, addressing everything from corrosion and leaks to clogs and breaks, restoring your plumbing system's integrity and efficiency.

  • Restore full water pressure and consistent flow.
  • Prevent future leaks and water damage.
  • Extend the lifespan of your entire plumbing system.
  • Ensure a safe and reliable water supply throughout your home.

Efficient Water Heater Installation

Enjoy consistent hot water on demand with our professional water heater installation services. Whether you're upgrading to an energy-efficient model or replacing an old, failing unit, we ensure proper sizing, precise installation, and optimal performance for years of reliable hot water. We specialize in both traditional tank and modern tankless water heaters.

  • Consistent and reliable hot water supply for your family.
  • Reduced energy bills with efficient new models.

  • Expert advice on the best water heater solution for your home.
